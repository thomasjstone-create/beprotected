Privacy Policy

UK Lead Generation Privacy Notice

Introduction

Be Protected Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect personal data when you request a quote or otherwise interact with our forms and services in the UK.

Data We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, email and telephone number.
  • Insurance-related details including smoking status, date of birth, health history and homeownership.
  • Technical information such as IP address and browser type when you access our website.
  • Any other information you provide to support your quote request.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and manage your life insurance quote.
  • To contact you about your quote and related services.
  • To share information with insurance providers and comparison partners where necessary to fulfil your request.
  • To improve our service and comply with legal obligations.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the UK GDPR on the basis of:

  • Consent, when you submit your details for a quote.
  • Contractual necessity, to deliver the requested insurance quote.
  • Legitimate interests, to manage and improve our service.
  • Legal obligations, where required by law or regulation.

Who We Share Your Data With

We may share your data with:

  • Insurance providers and brokers to obtain quotes and underwriting information.
  • Service providers who support our operations, such as hosting, analytics, and security.
  • Regulatory authorities or law enforcement when required by law.

Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the quote, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Typically, contact and quote data is retained for up to 2 years, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of data where there is no lawful reason to keep it.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Request portability of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational steps to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Cookies and Tracking

We only use essential cookies required for the service and keep tracking to a minimum. No third-party marketing cookies are used without consent.
